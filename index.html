Welcome to Dark Country Boy - Haunted Blues Music

Dark Country Boy creates haunted blues music that lingers long after the last note fades.

Dark Country Boy is an independent recording artist whose music defies easy categorization. Drawing from the deep wells of American blues, outlaw country, Americana, and folk tradition, Dark Country Boy creates music that is raw, honest, and emotionally powerful.

Fire in the Blood - Album

The debut album "Fire in the Blood" is a landmark collection of haunted blues music. Every track tells a story — sometimes painful, sometimes triumphant, always real.
